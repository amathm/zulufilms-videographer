About

We are a media company that specializes in all things video. We work to meet your needs ensuring that we create something that we can both be proud of.

Zulu Films believe in authenticity, honesty, creativity, integrity and hard work. These beliefs are the foundation to our processes and creative approaches to the content we create. Helping our clients engage with their audiences and achieve measurable results.

We are moving image experts, working with brands and business to create brave and strategic creative content solutions. A big part of our creative process is collaboration; we are always to open to new ideas and want to push boundaries and expand our experiences.
